Zaylee Ocean

Jewelry by a girl who loves the sea

What Zaylee Makes

Zaylee makes unique jewelry from sea glass she finds herself:

  • 🌊 Necklaces – made with sea-polished glass on simple cords
  • 🐚 Bracelets – fun, colorful pieces with a beachy vibe
  • 💍 Rings – tiny treasures, each one different
Every piece is handmade and one-of-a-kind.

Why Zaylee Does This

Zaylee gives 50% of the profits to charities that help the ocean: turtle rescue, beach cleanups, and protecting sea animals.

How It Started

It all began with beach walks and pockets full of glass treasures.

  • 💡 Zaylee called them “mermaid jewels”
  • 🎁 She wanted to make gifts from them
  • 🌱 She decided to give half the money to help the sea
